Laser Cutting

Earrings

These earrings were one of my first laser cutting projects. I designed them in Adobe Illustrator and cut them out of medium density fiberboard (MDF). The design is based on a pair of earrings that I found online that I really liked, but I wanted to make them myself and add a bit of my own flair to them.

Coasters

These coasters were cut to be identical and able to be appreciated individually, but to also be appreciated as a full set of four. The design is based on a flower pattern that can be cut into four equal pieces and then placed together to form a full flower. I designed the pattern in Adobe Illustrator and cut it out of medium density fiberboard (MDF). The unequal prior burning on the pieces from previous projects adds a bit of character to the pieces and makes them more unique, which I really like.
